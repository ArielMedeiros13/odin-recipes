Lazy Pizza

Description

Lazy Pizza is the ultimate survival recipe for developers, students, and anyone who wants pizza without emotional commitment to cooking.

It uses simple ingredients, minimal preparation, and delivers maximum happiness per minute invested. Perfect for late-night coding sessions or pretending you have your life together.

Ingredients

  • 1 pre-made pizza dough or flatbread
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and oregano
  • Any toppings you find in the fridge (ham, tomato, onion, etc.)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C, if you have a weak oven, just... do what you can..
  2. Place the dough or flatbread on a baking tray. Please, have a baking tray.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly. Go hard on this, like Michelangelo!
  4. Add mozzarella cheese. Or the old one you have back on the fridge.
  5. Throw random toppings responsibly. Unless...
  6. Drizzle olive oil (or the oil on your forehead) and sprinkle oregano.
  7. Bake for 10–15 minutes until cheese melts.
  8. Remove, slice, pretend you worked hard and that's your whole prize.
