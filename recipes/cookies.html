Not an Italian Cookie

Description

Not an Italian Cookie are perfect for when you want something sweet but refuse to start a full baking operation. Minimal ingredients, minimal effort, maximum reward.

These cookies are soft, simple, and beginner-friendly. Ideal for late-night cravings or celebrating that your HTML finally works and you are all of the sudden our new Steve Jobs.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup peanut butter (or butter) (or... anything that might work)
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 egg (see if it's not rotten, please)
  • Chocolate chips (optional but morally correct)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix peanut butter, sugar, and egg in a bowl. Use your hands. I'm serious. Just do it.
  3. Add chocolate chips, if you have some in this dungeon.
  4. Form small balls and place on baking tray.
  5. Flatten slightly with a fork. Come on, we are not barbarians, right?
  6. Bake for 10–12 minutes.
  7. Let cool for a few minutes.
  8. Eat and feel productive.
